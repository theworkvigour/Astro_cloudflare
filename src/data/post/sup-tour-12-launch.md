### Go Farther with SUP Tour 12

Wavefella is proud to announce the launch of the SUP Tour 12, a dedicated touring inflatable SUP board designed for paddlers who want to cover distance without sacrificing portability.

The SUP Tour 12 features a pointed displacement hull that cuts through water efficiently, a 30-inch width that balances speed with stability, and a reinforced drop-stitch core that inflates to a rigid, hard-board feel at 12-15 PSI.

**Key Features:**

- **12 ft length** — momentum retention and glide for long-distance paddling
- **Displacement hull** — pointed nose for wave cutting and straight-line tracking
- **30-inch width** — optimal balance of touring speed and stability
- **Drop-stitch core** — military-grade PVC construction at just 20 lbs
- **Complete touring kit** — includes backpack, pump, touring fin, leash, and repair kit

The SUP Tour 12 is available now at $749, complete with all accessories needed to hit the water immediately. It joins the Wavefella lineup alongside the popular SUP Explorer 11 all-around board.

For more information, visit the SUP Tour 12 product page or contact your Wavefella dealer.
