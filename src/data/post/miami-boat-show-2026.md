### Miami Bound

Wavefella will exhibit at the 2026 Miami International Boat Show, February 11-15 at the Miami Marine Stadium Park and Basin. This marks Wavefella's debut at North America's largest boat show.

The Wavefella booth (B-312) will feature the full product lineup with hands-on demonstrations:

- **RIB 330** — 10.8 ft compact RIB for tenders and recreational use
- **RIB 450 Patrol** — 14.8 ft rescue-ready RIB with reinforced hull
- **SUP Tour 12** — touring inflatable SUP with displacement hull
- **SUP Explorer 11** — all-around beginner SUP
- **Kayak Tandem** — award-winning two-person inflatable kayak
- **Life Vest Pro** — SOLAS-certified automatic life vest

On-water demos will be available by appointment, allowing attendees to test Wavefella RIBs and SUPs on the Venetian Basin. Wavefella executives will also be available for B2B meetings with potential dealers and fleet operators.

"We are thrilled to bring Wavefella to the Miami Boat Show audience," said the Wavefella sales director. "The show attracts serious boaters and marine professionals who appreciate quality construction and thoughtful design."

Visit wavefella.com/miami-boat-show to schedule a meeting or book an on-water demo.
