### Award-Winning Family Fun

Paddle Magazine has named the Wavefella Kayak Tandem the Best Family Watercraft of 2026, praising its stability, thoughtful dual-cockpit layout, and easy portability.

"In a field of family watercraft, the Kayak Tandem stands out for getting the fundamentals right," the review states. "Two separate cockpits with adjustable seats, a 36-inch beam that inspires confidence, and the ability to pack the whole thing into a backpack when you are done."

The Kayak Tandem accommodates two paddlers with a combined load capacity of 400 lbs. Its 12-foot length provides efficient tracking, while the drop-stitch floor creates a rigid platform that rivals hard-shell kayaks.

"The family market has unique requirements," said the Wavefella product team. "You need stability for nervous first-timers, room for gear, and a setup that doesn't require a roof rack. The Kayak Tandem delivers all three."

The Kayak Tandem is available now at $649, complete with pump, backpack, adjustable seats, and repair kit — everything needed for a family day on the water.
