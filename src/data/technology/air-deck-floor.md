The Air Deck floor is a high-pressure inflatable system used in Wavefella dinghies (AirDeck 270, AirDeck 360). It replaces traditional plywood or aluminum slat floors, reducing overall weight by up to 40% and cutting setup time in half.

While the air deck flexes more than a solid wood floor, it provides adequate rigidity for standing, fishing, and walking while enabling the entire boat to deflate into a compact transport package.
