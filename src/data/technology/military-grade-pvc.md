Wavefella uses multi-layer laminated PVC fabric rated for demanding marine environments. Each layer is fusion-welded rather than glued, eliminating the delamination risk found in lower-quality inflatables.

The material is tested for UV resistance, saltwater corrosion, and abrasion — ensuring long-term durability in coastal, lake, and river environments.
