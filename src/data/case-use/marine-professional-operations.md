Professional marine operations including law enforcement patrol, search and rescue, and commercial transport rely on Wavefella's RIB range. The RIB 330 provides coastal transport capability while the RIB 450 Patrol is engineered for extreme conditions with a self-bailing deck, reinforced deep-V hull, and 60 HP engine capacity.

All professional operations require SOLAS-compliant safety equipment including the Life Vest Pro with automatic hydrostatic inflation.
