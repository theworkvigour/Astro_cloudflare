Coastal touring and exploration demand boards that track straight, glide efficiently between strokes, and handle moderate chop. The displacement hull design of the SUP Tour 12' cuts through water for efficient long-distance paddling.

Touring paddlers typically cover 5-15 km per session, exploring coastlines, navigating between beaches, and accessing remote coves.
