## Adventure in a backpack.

The Kayak Lite is built for travelers and outdoor enthusiasts who want a real kayak that fits in a backpack. At 15 lbs, it is light enough to carry miles to remote launch spots.

### Design

- 9 ft length for maneuverability on rivers and lakes
- 34" width provides excellent primary stability
- 3 independent air chambers for safety redundancy
- Removable skeg for directional tracking

### What's included

- Kayak with drop-stitch floor
- Adjustable seat with backrest
- Dual-action hand pump
- Removable skeg
- Travel backpack
- Repair kit

### Best for

- Solo river and lake touring
- Backpacking and camping adventures
- Airline travel and overseas trips
