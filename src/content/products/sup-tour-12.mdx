The SUP Tour 12 is built for paddlers who want to cover distance. Its longer length and pointed displacement hull cut through water efficiently, while the 30" width balances speed with stability.

- 12 ft length for momentum retention and glide
- Displacement hull with pointed nose for wave cutting
- 30" width balances touring speed with stability

- Repair kit

- Long-distance lake and coastal touring
