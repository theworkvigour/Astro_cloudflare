## Inflate faster. Deflate smarter.

The Dual-Action Pump moves air on both the push and pull strokes, cutting inflation time in half compared to single-action pumps. The built-in pressure gauge ensures you hit the right PSI every time.

### Design

- Dual-action piston moves air in both directions
- Built-in analog pressure gauge
- Non-slip base for stable operation
- Ergonomic handle reduces hand fatigue

### What's included

- Dual-action pump with hose
- Pressure gauge (built-in)
- 3 valve adapters: Halkey-Roberts, Boston, leaf-valve

### Best for

- Inflating SUP boards to 12-15 PSI
- Kayak and dinghy inflation
- Travel and remote launch site use
