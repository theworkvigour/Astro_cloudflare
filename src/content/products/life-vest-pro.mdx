## Professional-grade protection.

The Life Vest Pro is designed for commercial mariners, rescue professionals, and offshore workers who need SOLAS-compliant flotation. Its automatic hydrostatic mechanism deploys only when submerged, keeping it low-profile during normal wear.

### Design

- Automatic hydrostatic inflation on immersion at 0.5-1.0 m depth
- Backup manual inflation tube
- SOLAS-grade reflective tape for maximum visibility
- Integrated spray hood for rough conditions
- Waterproof light for nighttime visibility
- Lifting harness attachment point

### What's included

- Life vest with hydrostatic mechanism
- CO2 cylinder (installed)
- Spray hood
- Waterproof light
- Whistle
- Manual inflation tube
- Storage bag

### Best for

- Offshore commercial operations
- Search and rescue personnel
- Law enforcement marine units
- Commercial fishing and shipping
