### Certified for the High Seas

The Wavefella Life Vest Pro has received full SOLAS (Safety of Life at Sea) and MED (Marine Equipment Directive) certification, qualifying it for commercial marine applications worldwide.

The Life Vest Pro features automatic hydrostatic inflation that deploys only on immersion at 0.5-1.0 meter depth, keeping the vest low-profile during normal wear. It includes a spray hood, waterproof light, lifting harness attachment point, and SOLAS-grade reflective tape.

"SOLAS certification is the gold standard for marine safety equipment," said the Wavefella product safety lead. "This opens our life vest to commercial shipping fleets, offshore fishing vessels, and maritime authorities who require certified equipment by regulation."

The Life Vest Pro is priced at $299 and available immediately. It joins the Life Vest Classic (50N+ CE/ISO 12402 recreational vest) in the Wavefella safety lineup.

Commercial buyers can purchase through the Wavefella website or contact the B2B sales team for fleet pricing.
