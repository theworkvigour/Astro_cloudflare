### Ready for Spring

As water temperatures rise and daylight extends, paddlers across the country are preparing for the 2026 season. Here is your spring checklist.

**Inspect Your Board:** Check for cracks, punctures, or UV damage. Pay special attention to seam areas on inflatable boards. Wavefella drop-stitch boards should be inspected at the valve connection and rail edges.

**Check Your Pump:** Ensure the pressure gauge reads accurately. Lubricate the O-ring on your pump barrel with silicone grease. Test both the inflation and deflation functions on dual-action pumps.

**Refresh Safety Gear:** Replace CO2 cylinders on inflatable life vests. Check that whistles are present and functional. Inspect leash webbing for fraying.

**Update Your Kit:** Spring is a good time to add a dry bag, waterproof phone case, or upgrade to a carbon paddle for the season ahead.

Whether you are paddling a SUP Explorer 11 on a calm lake or a Kayak Lite on a river, starting the season with properly maintained gear makes every outing safer and more enjoyable.
